Happy Diwali!

As we celebrate the festival of lights, may the bonds we forged at JNV Jalna continue to shine brighter with each passing year. From morning assemblies to late-night hostel stories, may this Diwali rekindle all the beautiful memories of our incredible journey together!

Our Journey Timeline

2017 - The Beginning

We first stepped into JNV Jalna as young, curious students, beginning our 7-year journey together.

2018 - Settling In

We adapted to the hostel life, made new friends, and started understanding the JNV culture.

2019 - Finding Our Path

We discovered our interests, joined different clubs, and started shaping our personalities.

2020 - The Pandemic Year

We faced challenges with online classes but stayed connected as a batch.

2021 - Back to Campus

We returned to school with renewed energy and appreciation for our time together.

2022 - Senior Year Begins

We took on leadership roles and started preparing for our future.

2023 - Final Year

We cherished every moment, knowing our time together was coming to an end.

2024 - Graduation

We bid farewell to JNV Jalna, carrying memories that will last a lifetime.
